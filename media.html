Experience

In the world of photography and film

We've been passionate about photography for over 15 years, and for over 10, we've been creating films that delight and inspire. Our work encompasses thousands of completed projects and countless pieces that have garnered acclaim worldwide. Our photos have appeared in prestigious magazines, and our music videos have aired on Polish television programs such as Dzień Dobry TVN. One of our standout achievements is the production of a music video for Szymon Wydra and Carpe Diem, which garnered widespread acclaim.

We've also had the honor of working with numerous renowned artists and brands, such as Orville Richard Burrell, known as Shaggy – the artist behind hits like "It Wasn't Me" and "Boombastic." Our portfolio also includes advertising projects for global brands like Carlsberg, as well as collaborations with jewelers and renowned clothing brands.

Our experience, professionalism, and creativity ensure that every project is executed to the highest standard, exceeding our clients' expectations.

Collaboration Process

Working with us is a simple, transparent, and effective process that allows you to maximize your time and our creativity. Here's how it works:
  1. Initial Contact - Contact us via the form, email, or phone. We'll initially discuss your needs, vision, and expectations, as well as available dates.
  2. Consultation and Quote - Based on your requirements, we prepare a detailed project plan and quote. We determine the scope of services, schedule, and all the details of the collaboration.
  3. Preparation for production - We help in preparing for the session or photo shoot – we advise on location, styling and script.
  4. Project Implementation - We begin shooting or recording within the agreed timeframe. Our team pays attention to every detail to ensure the final result meets your expectations.
  5. Post-production - Finished materials undergo professional processing – from photo retouching to video editing – to achieve the perfect result.
  6. Material Delivery - We deliver final materials in your chosen format and within agreed deadlines. We can also offer photo prints, albums, and other forms of presenting the results of our work.
