Moto Trip Rules

  • Overtaking prohibited
  • You must obey the orderly commands (one at the head and one at the end)
  • Stopping only when necessary and after informing the traffic warden
  • Wearing full protective equipment is mandatory
  • Speeding is prohibited
  • It is not possible to change the route once it has started
  • Failure to comply with the rules will result in the trip being interrupted.
  • In case of overnight stay – no riding on a motorcycle without a group
