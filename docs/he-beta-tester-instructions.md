# HE Beta Tester Instructions

This is an invite-only Hebrew beta. It is not a public launch.

## How To Enter

1. Sign in with the email address that was invited to the beta.
2. Open the beta link provided by the CyprusEye team, normally:
   `https://www.cypruseye.com/?lang=he`
3. Confirm the page is in Hebrew and the layout is right-to-left.
4. If the page stays English, tell the team your account email and the exact
   URL you opened.

Do not use `?ce_he_preview=1`; hidden preview is disabled during invite-only
beta.

Current tester account: `lilkangoomedia@gmail.com`.

## What To Check

Please test:

- Homepage.
- Blog posts sent by the team.
- Map, POI cards and recommendation cards.
- Trip, hotel and car pages sent by the team.
- Transport form with the selected beta locations.
- Basic booking labels, validation and summaries.

Report:

- Missing Hebrew text.
- English fallback that feels confusing.
- Broken right-to-left layout.
- Text overlapping buttons/cards.
- Empty cards, `undefined`, `null` or broken labels.
- Console errors if you know how to capture them.

## What Not To Test

Do not test:

- Shop.
- Cart.
- Checkout.
- Shipping.
- Payments.
- Public `/he/` routes.
- SEO, sitemap, hreflang or indexing.

These are outside the first Hebrew beta.

## Known Limitations

- Some dynamic content may intentionally fall back to English.
- Only selected blog, POI, recommendation, trip, hotel, car and transport
  records are part of the beta.
- Hebrew SEO is disabled.
- `/he/` URLs are not public.
- Shop is not available as a Hebrew flow.

## How To Report Bugs

Send the team:

- Page URL.
- Screenshot or short screen recording.
- What you expected.
- What happened.
- Device and browser.
- Whether you were signed in.
