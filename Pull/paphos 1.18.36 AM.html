Paphos
A place of myths, monuments, and beautiful beaches

Paphos is a picturesque city on the western coast of Cyprus, full of history, mythology, and beautiful beaches. It attracts both culture lovers and those seeking seaside relaxation. Here are five reasons why it is worth spending your holidays in Paphos.

Archaeological heritage

Paphos is one of the most important archaeological sites in Cyprus. The city is listed as a UNESCO World Heritage Site, and its ancient ruins attract countless visitors. Be sure to visit the Archaeological Park of Paphos, where you can see the Tombs of the Kings, the ancient necropolis, and extraordinary mosaics in the Houses of Dionysos.

Greek mythology

Paphos is intertwined with Greek mythology and the legends of Aphrodite, the goddess of love and beauty. Just outside the city stands Aphrodite’s Rock, considered the goddess’s birthplace. According to legend, swimming in the waters around the rock brings eternal youth and beauty. It’s a magical, romantic spot for couples and mythology enthusiasts alike.

Old Town charm

The enchanting Old Town of Paphos dazzles with its traditional character and narrow lanes. You’ll find classic houses, cafés, taverns, and small shops that offer an authentic taste of Cypriot life. Wandering through the Old Town feels like stepping back in time.

Beautiful beaches

Paphos is famous for its stunning beaches that attract visitors from around the world. You’ll find sandy stretches perfect for sunbathing and relaxation, as well as rocky bays ideal for snorkeling and exploring marine life. Coral Bay is among the most popular nearby spots, celebrated for its white sand and crystal-clear water.

Culture and festivals

Paphos hosts numerous festivals and cultural events throughout the year. Don’t miss the annual Paphos Aphrodite Festival, a theatrical celebration featuring opera and ballet performances. It’s the perfect opportunity to immerse yourself in art and culture in a truly magical setting.

Paphos has so much to offer visitors. Whether you’re fascinated by history and mythology or simply want to unwind on gorgeous beaches, the city delivers. It blends cultural heritage with natural beauty to create unforgettable holiday memories.
